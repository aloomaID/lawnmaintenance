Globe Arizona experiences a subtropical climate, characterized by hot, humid summers and mild winters. This climate supports a variety of grass types, including Bermuda, Zoysia, and St. Augustine, which thrive in warm temperatures and can withstand the occasional drought.
2. Soil Health:
The quality of your lawn starts with the soil. Conducting a soil test can provide insights into pH levels and nutrient deficiencies. In Globe Arizona sandy soils are common, which may require regular amendments with organic matter to improve water retention and nutrient availability.
3. Watering Practices:
Effective irrigation is vital in Globe Arizona due to the region's warm climate. Lawns typically require 1 to 1.5 inches of water per week. Early morning watering is recommended to minimize evaporation and fungal diseases.

Common pests in Globe Arizona such as grubs and chinch bugs, can damage your lawn. Integrated Pest Management (IPM) strategies, including regular monitoring and the use of eco-friendly treatments, can effectively control these pests while protecting beneficial insects.

Globe, AZ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Globe (Western Apache: Bésh Baa Gowąh 'Place of Metal') is a city in Gila County, Arizona, United States. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 7,249. The city is the county seat of Gila County. Globe was founded c. 1875 as a mining camp. Mining, tourism, government and retirees are most important in the present-day Globe economy.
