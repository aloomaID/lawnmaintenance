East Lake Florida experiences a subtropical climate, characterized by hot, humid summers and mild winters. This climate supports a variety of grass types, including Bermuda, Zoysia, and St. Augustine, which thrive in warm temperatures and can withstand the occasional drought.
2. Soil Health:
The quality of your lawn starts with the soil. Conducting a soil test can provide insights into pH levels and nutrient deficiencies. In East Lake Florida sandy soils are common, which may require regular amendments with organic matter to improve water retention and nutrient availability.
3. Watering Practices:
Effective irrigation is vital in East Lake Florida due to the region's warm climate. Lawns typically require 1 to 1.5 inches of water per week. Early morning watering is recommended to minimize evaporation and fungal diseases.

Common pests in East Lake Florida such as grubs and chinch bugs, can damage your lawn. Integrated Pest Management (IPM) strategies, including regular monitoring and the use of eco-friendly treatments, can effectively control these pests while protecting beneficial insects.

East Lake, FL Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

East Lake-Orient Park is an unincorporated census-designated place in Hillsborough County, Florida, United States. The population was 22,753 at the 2010 census, up from 5,703 at the 2000 census following an expansion of its borders. The area is the home of the Florida State Fairgrounds, which hosts the Florida State Fair in mid-February of each year. It was previously home to East Lake Square Mall. The ZIP code for East Lake-Orient Park is 33610 (East Lake) and 33619 (Orient Park).
