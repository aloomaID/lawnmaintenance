The quality of your lawn starts with the soil. Conducting a soil test can provide insights into pH levels and nutrient deficiencies. In Waynesville Missouri sandy soils are common, which may require regular amendments with organic matter to improve water retention and nutrient availability.

Effective irrigation is vital in Waynesville Missouri due to the region's warm climate. Lawns typically require 1 to 1.5 inches of water per week. Early morning watering is recommended to minimize evaporation and fungal diseases.
